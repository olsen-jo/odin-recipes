Mirelurk Egg Omelette

Quick and easy breakfast omelette, made from the egg of the wretched Mirelurk. Restores +50 AP!

Ingredients

  • 1 vessel dirty water
  • 1 Mirelurk egg

Steps

  1. Whisk egg and dirty water until fully blended and frothy
  2. Preheat your skillet to 375 F
  3. Set cooking station to medium-low and slowly pour egg mixture into skillet
  4. Flip when underside has lightly browned
  5. For a real treat, add in chopped Molerat chunks or Radscorpion bits!
