Radstag Stew

This homemade dish is a delectable blend of Wasteland vegetables, Radstag meat and grain alcohol. +30 energy resist for 1 hour!

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs Radstag meat
  • 1 oz gourd
  • 2 oz silt bean, fresh or tinned
  • 16 fl oz vodka

Steps

  1. Chop gourd and silt bean into medium sized chunks
  2. Chunk and rinse Radstag meat and innards
  3. Combine all ingredients in pot, heat on med-high til lightly browned
  4. Turn down heat to low and simmer for 4-6 hours
  5. Slowly stir in vodka and simmer until juices thicken and vegetables are soft
